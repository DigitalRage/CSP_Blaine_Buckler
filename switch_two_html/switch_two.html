Switch 2

Image from: Stuff TV

Stats

The Switch 2 is rumored to have the CPU processing power of a **PS4 Pro** and **12GB** of RAM. The LED screen will go up to **1080p (Full HD)** and run at a max **120 FPS**. When docked, the Switch 2 will be capable of running games in **4K**. It is **one inch bigger** than the original and the controllers are **magnetic**. The SL and SR buttons are now **three times bigger**, and the Joy-Cons can be used as **computer mice**.

Games

The Switch 2 will launch with many titles, including:

  • Mario Kart World
    An open-world Mario Kart where you can free-roam or compete in a Grand Prix.
  • Donkey Kong Bonanza
    With the power of the Switch 2, Donkey Kong can now **break terrain** and dig almost anywhere he wants. He finds a rock-like friend that helps him mine **crystallized bananas**.
  • Hyrule Warriors: Age of Imprisonment
    A game exploring events in Tears of the Kingdom from Zelda's point of view.
  • Final Fantasy VII Integrated
    Experience the first part of the beloved **Final Fantasy VII** story, **remade in 4K graphics**.
  • Kirby Air Riders
    Relive the classic **Kirby Air Ride** with **new features** and modern improvements.

New Features

Image from: The Shortcut

New Features Include:

  • Game Chat
    Allows voice chat, screen sharing, **camera integration** (camera sold separately), and easier **co-op play**.
  • Local Play (Available for Certain Games)
    Some games will allow **wireless local multiplayer** with: **Switch 2, Switch, Switch Lite, and Switch OLED models**.
  • New USB-C Port
    Located on the top for **charging in tabletop mode**, **wired controller connections**, and **camera attachments**.
  • Enhanced Speakers
    Upgraded **stereo sound** for **better in-game audio**.
