Stats

The Switch 2 is rumored to have the CPU processing power of a PS4 Pro and 12GB of RAM. The LED screen supports 1080p (Full HD) with a maximum refresh rate of 120 FPS. When docked, the Switch 2 can run games in 4K resolution. It is one inch larger than the original Switch, and its magnetic controllers provide an improved grip. The SL and SR buttons have tripled in size, and the Joy-Cons now function as computer mice.

Games

The Switch 2 will launch with a variety of exciting titles, including:

  • Mario Kart World
    A fully open-world Mario Kart game where players can freely explore or compete in Grand Prix races.
  • Donkey Kong Bonanza
    Donkey Kong can now break terrain and dig anywhere he chooses. He befriends a rock-like companion who helps him collect crystallized bananas.
  • Hyrule Warriors: Age of Imprisonment
    Experience the events of Tears of the Kingdom from Zelda's perspective, uncovering hidden lore.
  • Final Fantasy VII Integrated
    The first chapter of the beloved Final Fantasy VII story, remastered in 4K graphics with a modernized narrative approach.
  • Kirby Air Riders
    A remake of the classic Kirby Air Ride, updated with new modes and enhanced gameplay mechanics.

Key Features

  • Improved Display: The Switch 2 boasts a larger 7.9-inch 1080p screen with HDR support and a refresh rate of up to 120Hz, providing vibrant visuals and smooth gameplay.
  • Enhanced Performance: Powered by Nvidia's Tegra T239 chip, the console features advanced processing capabilities, including 12GB of LPDDR5 RAM and a custom Ampere-based GPU.
  • 4K Output: When docked, the Switch 2 can deliver 4K resolution for TV gaming.
  • Magnetic Joy-Con Attachments: The new Joy-Con 2 controllers attach magnetically and can function as computer mice in compatible games.
  • GameChat: A new feature allowing voice and video chat, screen sharing, and multiplayer communication via the "C Button" on the right Joy-Con.
